Challenge

A brand that carries
two centuries of trust.

Any agency can claim modernity. Few can claim 186 years. The risk was losing what made Abreu irreplaceable: the weight of generations, the warmth of family. Contemporary without being rootless.

Strategy

Continuity, not
rupture.

The process began not with mood boards but with archives — eight years of research documented in Agência Abreu: Uma Viagem de 175 Anos (2015). Forms, colours and structures drawn from 186 years of visual memory.

Positioning: heritage as competitive advantage. Not nostalgia — authority.

The Resende Connection

01
"Augusto de Abreu sempre me acompanhou na trajetória da pintura." Júlio Resende · 1917–2011
The Fresco · 1959

Commissioned for the Aliados renovation by architect Viana de Lima, Resende's entrance fresco (1959) became a landmark of modernist patronage in Porto.

The Suite Abreu

Augusto Abreu acquired 23 of Resende's paintings — the Suite Abreu. Their forms, colours and textures became the visual foundation of the new identity.

Catalogue covers

Resende designed Abreu travel catalogue covers in 1972, '73, '75 and '96 — one of the most sustained artist–patron relationships in 20th-century Portuguese art.

Source Sousa, F. & Pereira, M. C. M. Agência Abreu — Uma Viagem de 175 Anos · 2015
