Whistleblowing Platform

A compliance platform that feels as trustworthy as it needs to be.

CROW is a whistleblowing channel platform built to comply with EU Directive 2019/1937 and Portuguese Law 93/2021. Any company with 50+ employees is legally required to have one. The design challenge: make something institutional feel approachable — and make something sensitive feel completely safe.

The UI covers two distinct surfaces: a public-facing report portal and a private back-office for case managers. Both had to work under the same visual system while serving radically different user needs.
