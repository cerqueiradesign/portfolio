A digital face for
Portugal's most refined
window systems.

Lummenn is a premium Portuguese glazing brand — Lummenn 30, 40, 60, 70 and Canyon systems installed in some of the country's most prestigious residential projects. The challenge: translate that physical precision and material beauty into a digital experience.

The website needed to feel as considered as the products themselves — minimal, confident, and architectural in its rhythm. Every screen, component, and interaction was designed to reflect the brand's core values of ultra-minimalism, durability, and light.
