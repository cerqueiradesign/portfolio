3D Illustrations
— the project.

Character and scene illustrations inspired by pop culture — Grogu from The Mandalorian, Simba from The Lion King, Hagrid, Doraemon, Stitch, and more. Each piece built entirely in Blender.

These illustrations were a creative exercise in translating 2D characters and iconography into three-dimensional space with realistic lighting, materials and rendering. A direct expression of personal aesthetic sensibility.
